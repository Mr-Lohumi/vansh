I. Heirloom Recipes
Savitri's Dal Makhani
Passed down by Savitri Sharma (Generation 0)
This recipe requires patience. The urad dal must be soaked overnight and slow-cooked over a low flame for exactly 12 hours. The secret ingredient added by Savitri ji is a pinch of roasted kasuri methi harvested directly from the ancestral village in Uttarakhand.

It is a staple served during every major alliance gathering in the network.
II. Annual Pooja Protocol
The Kuldevi (Ancestral Deity) Pooja is strictly observed on the first Tuesday of Navratri every year. The Patriarch of the family must lead the ceremony following these steps:
  1. Purification of the ancestral altar using Ganga jal.
  2. Lighting of the Akhand Jyot (eternal lamp) using pure ghee.
  3. Offering of the red chunri and fresh marigolds.
  4. Recitation of the lineage Gotra (Kashyap) to invoke ancestral blessings.
